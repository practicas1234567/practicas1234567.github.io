Fixed Rent
Fixed rent refers to a set amount of money paid regularly for the use of a property or asset. The rent remains constant over a specified period, typically for the duration of a lease agreement.
Variable Rent
Variable rent, also known as adjustable rent, fluctuates based on predetermined factors such as changes in market conditions, inflation rates, or the performance of the leased property. The rent amount can vary from one period to another.
Mixed Rent
Mixed rent, also called hybrid rent, combines elements of both fixed and variable rent structures. It may include a base fixed amount along with additional variable components, providing flexibility while ensuring a degree of stability in rental payments.
